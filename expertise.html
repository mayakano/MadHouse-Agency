    Strategy

    We approach Strategy as an Organizational Architecture first. A visual schematic. A blueprint that defines the model, each of its products, units or entities, and its common denominator. This serves as the foundation from which all planning, development and execution come. Equal parts art and science, for us, Strategy is the intersection of insights and vision. We believe enterprise value ultimately comes from an organic purpose that transcends everything you do.

    Our Strategic Planning Practice includes market analysis, trend and cultural assessments, strategic mapping and brand positioning, product development, monetization, organic and non-organic growth areas.

    Brand
    Platforms

    We believe the brand is a platform itself. It interacts with the world. It develops relationships with customers. It is an emotional, evolving asset. It is the intagible and often most valuable aspect of an enterprise. Like all strong foundations, a solid brand platform is built on a higher purpose. It articulates what it stands for, how it behaves, why it exists, to whom it is relevant and why anyone should care. Our approach to brand platform development is to ultimately determine a brand's unique place in the world.

    Our Brand Platform Practice includes the development of an organizing idea, archetype, identity model, narrative and brand experience.

    Digital Marketing &
    Technology Design

    Deep learning and algorithmic brand development aren't tools for some distant future - they are happening now. Insight led by the ever growing data infrastructure and frictionless accessibility. We understand that Technology has the capacity to push our thinking, our boundaries, our culture. It helps organizations do new things, leverage competitive advantages and add value. It is transforamtive. However, technology itself, like data itself, has little meaning or application without purpose and appropriate integrations. And there is a difference between employing and exploiting technology for what it does well, and being an enterprise that creates technology.

    At Madhouse, we curate technology solutions that drive transactions. Because we believe technology has become a requisite in today's planning process. A vital element for future proofing. We view technology as a tool form which we drive insights that advance strategy, brand and creative. And we partner with companies and emerging technologies that build useful tech stacks for our clients.
